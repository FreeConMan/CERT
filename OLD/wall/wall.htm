Gay W. Wall spouse - Jack Wall

Gay holds a B.S. in Education from UNT where she belonged to Alpha Delta Pi Sorority. She is a former public school teacher with teaching fields in English and journalism sponsoring both newspaper and yearbook productions at Sherman High

School and at Irving High School.

For many years, she was an antiques dealer in Dallas, selling American primitive antiques.

Her main hobby is traditional rug hooking and she enters her rugs annually for judging at the State Fair of Texas, where she has won a dozen or so awards. Other hobbies include gardening, cooking and fishing with her three grandchildren.

She belongs to two book clubs, The Craig Class at the Dallas Woman’s Club, the Salvation Army Guild, and she is an Ambassador of the Pastoral Counseling and Education Center. She is a active member of Highland Park Presbyterian Church.
