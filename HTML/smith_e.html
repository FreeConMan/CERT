Nikki DeShazo, J.D. spouse - Ed Smith

Nikki retired as Judge of the Probate Court of Dallas County in 2009, after more than 26 years. She is a former Presiding Statu- tory Probate Judge of Texas and former President of the National College of Probate Judges.

She received a B.S. in nursing from Northwestern State College in Natchitoches, LA., and a J.D. from SMU.

She is a Life Fellow of the Texas Bar Foundation and a member of the Leadership Dallas Class of 1991. She is also a Lifetime Member of the UNT President’s Council, a member of the SMU School of Law Alumni Board and former President of the Executive Women of Dallas. Nikki is also an Honorary Alumnus of North Texas.

Nikki received the 2009-2010 Distinguished Alumni Award for Judicial Service from SMU Dedman School of Law.
