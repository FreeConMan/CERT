Company Information

The University of North Texas is a student-focused, public research university located in Denton. UNT is the nation’s 33rd largest public university and Texas’ fourth largest university. And as one of the most comprehensive universities in one of the largest regions in the nation, UNT attracts high-quality students to its offerings of 97 bachelor’s, 88 master’s and 40 doctoral degree programs in its 12 colleges and schools. UNT is the flagship of the UNT System.
