Meredith Dickenson spouse - Jerry Dickenson

Meredith Dickenson is the principal founder of Dickenson Communications, a small public relations agency that provides campaign planning, consulting, crisis communications, editorial services and media relations among its services.

Her clients have included LeTourneau University, Meadows Museum, Briggs-Freeman Real Estate Broker, Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce, LeTourneau University, Meadows Museum, Wake Forest University and the University of North Texas.

Before starting her practice, Dickenson was assistant vice president for communications at The University of Texas at Dallas from 2007 to 2010. Before that she worked at SMU as the director of national media relations. She has worked as a press secretary in the Texas Legislature, as an aide to the late Lieutenant Governor of Texas Bob Bullock, and as a senior writer and media relations specialist for the University of North Texas. She began her career as a television reporter for two CBS affiliates. She has a bachelor’s degree in journalism from UT Austin and a master’s degree in liberal arts studies from SMU.

Dickenson is a member of the Council for the Advancement and Support of Education (CASE) and the Public Relations Society of America.
