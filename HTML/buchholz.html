SWS Group Inc. is a publicly owned holding company with subsidiaries engaged in providing securities clearing, securities brokerage, investment baking and banking services. Mr. Buchholz cofounded Southwest Securities, now SWS Group’s principal subsidiary, as a Dallas-based securities broker/dealer and New York Stock Exchange member firm in 1972.
